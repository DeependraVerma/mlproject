Math's Marks Predictor

Welcome to the Math's Marks Predictor, where we leverage the power of machine learning to estimate math scores based on various factors. This innovative tool takes into account critical variables such as gender, race/ethnicity, parental level of education, lunch type, and completion of a test preparation course. By analyzing historical data containing math scores along with reading and writing scores, our model identifies patterns and trends to provide accurate predictions.

Whether you're a student, parent, or educator, understanding the factors that influence math performance can be valuable for academic planning and support. Our goal is to empower individuals with insights that contribute to a more informed and personalized educational experience.

To get started, explore the navigation links to access the prediction feature and discover how machine learning can assist in understanding and improving math scores.
